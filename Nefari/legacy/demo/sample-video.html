Azureus Rising (Replaced Sound Effects)

Date published: 2014-08-26

Author: Brian Hom

Azureus Rising is a short film originally produced by Black Sun Entertainment. The original can be found here. This version was redubbed with custom foley and sound effects to achieve the same or at least a similar level of quality to the original's sounds.

Video player is in alpha and may have both functional and graphical issues.
